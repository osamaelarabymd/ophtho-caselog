FAQ

How do I add EyeLog to my iPhone?
Open the app in Safari → tap the Share button (box with arrow) → tap Add to Home Screen → tap Add. The app icon appears on your home screen and works like a native app.
Can I edit a case after saving?
Yes. Go to Cases tab → find your case → tap the edit (pencil) icon. All fields are editable. Your changes sync to the cloud immediately.
How do I create a PDF for my PD?
Go to Log Case tab → scroll to the bottom → tap Export PDF for a complete case list, or Monthly Report for a concise month-by-month summary. Both are ready to share directly with your program director.
Is my data private?
Yes. Your cases are stored in a private Supabase database with row-level security — only your account can read your data. Your program director can only view what you explicitly share. Journal entries and notes are never shared.
How do I enable daily reminders?
On the Dashboard, tap the Reminders button in the hero banner → allow browser notifications when prompted → you'll receive a push reminder at 6 PM daily to log your cases.
How do I use voice input?
Tap the 🎙️ microphone icon next to any text field → speak your notes → the text appears automatically. Switch between EN and AR for Arabic transcription. Works in the case notes, journal entry, and to-do fields.
What is the ⌘K command palette?
Press ⌘K (Mac) or Ctrl+K (Windows/Android) anywhere in EyeLog to open the command palette — a Spotlight-style search that lets you jump to any tab, log a case, open a journal entry, or run a weekly review in one keystroke.
How do I use templates?
In Log Case, fill in the case form → tap + Save Template. Next time, tap the template button at the top of the form to auto-fill all fields instantly — perfect for your most common procedure types.
